# Code of Conduct

AI Freedom Trust Federation is built around human dignity, consent, practical repair, and accountable stewardship.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate with respect and clarity.
- Assume good faith while still asking precise questions.
- Critique ideas, implementations, and risks without attacking people.
- Keep discussions grounded in evidence, working systems, and shared goals.
- Respect privacy, consent, and boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, abuse, or demeaning language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Manipulative, coercive, or exploitative conduct.
- Misrepresenting project status, capabilities, credentials, or affiliations.
- Using federation projects to promote harm, fraud, or deceptive activity.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with these expectations.
